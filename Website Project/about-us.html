ABOUT STRONG-LOCK BRICKS

Strong-Lock Bricks

Strong-Lock Bricks are high-quality, durable construction materials designed to provide superior strength, stability, and longevity for building projects. Known for their interlocking design, these bricks ensure a secure and precise fit, reducing the need for mortar and speeding up construction time. Ideal for residential, commercial, and industrial applications, Strong-Lock Bricks combine innovation and reliability to meet modern construction demands while promoting efficiency and sustainability.

Brick Anatomy

Mortar Hole

A central hole designed to hold mortar securely, ensuring a stronger bond and alignment during construction.

Locking Male Part

A protruding element that interlocks with the adjacent brick's corresponding groove, creating a stable and precise fit, eliminating the need for excessive adhesives.

Insulating Hole

An internal cavity that enhances thermal and acoustic insulation while reducing the overall weight of the brick.

Standard Dimensions

With a width of 12.5 cm and a length of 25 cm, the brick is designed for modularity, making it ideal for a wide range of construction applications.

How are Strong-Lock Bricks made?

Strong-Lock Bricks are crafted using a mix of soil, cement, and water. This blend is placed in molds and subjected to high-pressure compaction, forming the bricks' interlocking design. The bricks are then cured to achieve their strength and durability, ensuring they meet the demands of modern construction.
